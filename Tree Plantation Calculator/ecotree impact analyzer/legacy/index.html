About This Platform

Tree Plantation Calculator

Tree Plantation Calculator is a comprehensive environmental sustainability platform designed to help individuals, institutions, NGOs, schools, colleges, and organizations estimate carbon absorption, evaluate plantation impact, analyze city air quality, discover suitable tree species, and understand the long-term environmental benefits of tree plantation through interactive calculators and visual analytics.

Built with research-grade environmental data, the platform supports smart-city planning, corporate CSR initiatives, educational projects, and NGO-driven reforestation campaigns across India.
